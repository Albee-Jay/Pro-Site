Who is Albert R Juarez?

I ask myself this question all the time and the fact that I'm a Gemini doesn't make it any easier to come up with a suitable answer.
So, here's a little bit about me ...

I love to laugh, eat, drink and be merry. Comedies (tv or movies) are my go-tos when I feel blah and I never say no to tacos, pasta or good beer.

When I'm not reading cards for my clients or studying to become an IT guru, I can be found listening to music: Dead or Alive, Prince, Cher and Lady Gaga are some my favorites.

I also dig reading, text books are great but I have to be in the mood for it, unless I'm studying a certain subject but I tend to lean toward vampire fiction, Anne Rice anyone?

I can't forget about watching horror movies, it is the absolute best and Michael, Jason & Freddy, are my buds.

I also have a pretty decent sized Batman collection, action figures, comic books and artwork.

But my most favorite thing ever is going dancing Friday nights at Mad Planet in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.


Oh yeah ... here are 3 things most people don't know about me: I am terrified of sharks, but I've never seen one up close (damn you Jaws); crayons and coloring books are my safe warm happy place and sometimes I am afraid of the dark.

Last but not least, my social media outlets are listed below. Some are for fun and being silly, some for sharing my interests, and others lean toward the business mind; but they all are about the human connection and creating meaningful relationships.

Facebook - Al Juarez

I've been on Facebook for a long time and for the most part I use it to connect with my family and friends ... and to be completely goofy and silly of course!

LinkedIn - Albert R Juarez

I just signed up for LinkedIn in September 2020 at the urging of an instructor from my IT class. I really like the way it works, it's actually a lot like FB but from a business end. I am looking forward to the connection I will make there and learning everything I can.

Instagram - @alstarot

Instagram is another to place to have fun with friends but here, I also get to work with my colleagues from the Tarot world and reach out to clients. For both work and play, it serves it's purpose.
